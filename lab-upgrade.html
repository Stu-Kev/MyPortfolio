Computer Laboratory Upgrade Project

Designed and proposed an upgraded network infrastructure for Manapla National High School, addressing issues with overcrowded devices, bandwidth congestion, and environmental risks to equipment. The project improved network performance, reliability, and safety to better support digital learning.

Role: Project Lead

Key Work:
- Designed new physical and logical network topologies for two labs.
- Proposed IP-addressing scheme, hardware upgrades (switches, Cat5 cabling, routers)
- Produced a budget plan with 5 percent contingency
- Created a 3D model of the network layout for a presentation
