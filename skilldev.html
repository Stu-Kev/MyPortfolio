Capstone Project: SkillDev

SkillDev is a web and mobile platform designed to address unemployment in Barangay Granada by providing accessible vocational training courses, progress tracking, and certifications. The project aligns with SDG #1 (No Poverty) and SDG #8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth) by supporting residents in gaining skills for employability.

Role: Project Manager

Key Work:
- Led a 3 member team using Agile SDLC with sprints and Gantt chart tracking.
- Coordinated design of course management, user progress, and certificate issuance modules
- Managed demos, ethical consent forms, and documentation
